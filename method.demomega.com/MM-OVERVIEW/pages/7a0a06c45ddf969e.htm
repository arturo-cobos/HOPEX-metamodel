BUILDING BLOCK - Business System Scenario

DescriptionAbstract Concept
A  Business System Scenario is a story involving the components of a Business System, interacting in order to produce Business Outcomes.
This includes:
1) A course of events represented by  Resource Flows depicting the steps towards the delivery of expected Business Outcomes.
2) Interacting Business Systems who participate to the story.
