ConceptDescriptionHOPEX Metaclass
  Assessment
An  Assessment is the result of the act of judging or assessing an aspect of the enterprise, based on an enterprise description and on the comparison of this description with the actual state of the enterprise.
An  Assessment is made by one or several Stakeholder s.
   Assessment Node
  Business Partner
A  Business Partner is a party who interacts with the enterprise in the context its operating eco-system (the Business Operating Ecosystem).
Example : Private Sector Customer, Regulation Authority,  Public health Authority.
   Business Partner
  Constraining Property
A  Constraining Property expresses a type of constraint that can be applied to Architecture Blocks.
These constraints can be either Measurable Property (Abstract) (Time, Mass, Cost, ..), Environmental Propertys (Terrain Type, Light conditions, Weather conditions) or a grouping of these (Set of Constraining Properties).
 Constraining Propertys are used to constrain Architecture Blocks in the context of non-functional requirement analysis and Value Proposition analysis.

  Endogenous Driver
Endogenous Drivers are those which subject of concern are within an enterprise that can impact its employment of Means or achievement of Ends.
    References:
  •  OMG - BMM - Internal Influencer
  Exogenous Driver
An  Exogenous Driver is an external Driver whose source of change is outside the enterprise’s organizational boundaries and that can impact its employment of Means or achievement of Ends.
    References:
  •  OMG - BMM - External Influencer
  Functional Asset
 Functional Assets encompasse Architecture Blocks used to describe how an enterprise function. This includes the purposes of an enterprise (Capability) and the way the enterprise operates to fulfill these purposes (Agents and their  Behaviors).
 Functional Assets include:
1. Blocks defining results of Behaviors of the enterprise or its sub-systems, that benefit to it internal or external customers : Outcome,
2. Blocks used to describe the capabilities of the enterprise or its sub-systems: Capability,
3. Blocks used to describe information: Information Object.
4. Blocks used to describe how the enterprise operates: Operating Assets (Agent, Process, Interaction Scenario, Service Interface).
All  Functional Assets are constrained by Policys and are exposed to Risk Types.
    References:

  Stakeholder
A  Stakeholder is an individual, team, organization, or classes thereof, having an interest in the enterprise.
 Stakeholders have concerns with respect to the enterprise considered in relation to its environment.
