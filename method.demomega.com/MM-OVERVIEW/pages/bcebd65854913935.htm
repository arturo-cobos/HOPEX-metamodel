BUILDING BLOCK - Business Partner

DescriptionAbstract Concept
A  Business Partner is a party who interacts with the enterprise in the context its operating eco-system (the Business Operating Ecosystem).
Example : Private Sector Customer, Regulation Authority,  Public health Authority.
VersionAvailable since: HOPEX V1R2
Last Update: HOPEX V1R2
Super Types  Business Operating Agent  
Definition Domain  EA Pattern - Business Operating Pattern
The EA Pattern - Business Operating Pattern domain defines concepts and relationships used to model how a Business Operating Agent operates/functions in its Operating Eco-System to produce Business Outcomes.
