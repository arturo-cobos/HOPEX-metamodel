BUILDING BLOCK - Control System

Description
A control system is a set of controls that ensure risk prevention and management, application of internal operating rules, respect a law or regulation, or work towards achievement of an objective as defined by company strategy. Examples: quality control system, control system relating to IT and Privacy, management control system, internal audit system.
