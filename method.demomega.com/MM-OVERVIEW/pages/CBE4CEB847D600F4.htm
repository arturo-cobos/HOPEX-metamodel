DEFINITION - Container

DescriptionA Container is a body-of-knowledge wich is made of a set of building-blocks that are asserted to exist for a given understanding of a business domain by a semantic community.
Building-blocks of a body-of-knowledge are said to be declared by their body-of-knowledge which plays a role of Container for its building-blocks.
