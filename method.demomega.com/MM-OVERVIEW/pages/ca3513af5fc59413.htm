BUILDING BLOCK - Environment Scenario

DescriptionAbstract Concept
An  Environment Scenario is a story that describes how an Agent interacts with its partners (Partner Agent) to achieve Outcomes in a specific Operating Eco-System (Agent Environment).
This includes:
1) A course of events represented by  Object Flows depicting the steps towards the delivery of expected Outcomes.
2) Interacting partners who participate to the story in the considered Operating Eco-System.
VersionAvailable since: HOPEX V2
Last Update: HOPEX V2
Super Types  Operating Eco-System  
Definition Domain  Operating Semantic
The Operating Semantic domain is used to model how a system (Agent) operates/functions (Behavior) in its Operating Eco-System to produce Outcomes that benefit (value) to other Agents or affect (have an effect upon) these Agents.
