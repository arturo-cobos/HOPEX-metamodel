CONCEPT DESCRIPTIONS

Abstract Concepts
ConceptDescriptionHOPEX Metaclass
  Behavioral Rule
A  Behavioral Rule is a Directive intended to guide the Behavior of Agents, in compliance with enterprise Policy(ies) or regulations.
Often, a rule is derived from a Policy.  Behavioral Rule are enforced in Processes and Agents.
    References:
  •  OMG - BMM - Business Rule
  •  OMG - SBVR - Business Rule (Behavioral)
 		  Rule Building Block
  Directive
A  Directive is an authoritative declaration that indicates how Agents and their Behaviors should be (or should not be) in the enterprise.
Specifically, a  Directive defines, constrains or liberates some aspects of an Agent and its Behaviors.
 Directives are intended to assert agent structures or to control or influence their Behaviors.  Directives are stated in declarative form.
A  Directive can either be a Policy or a Behavioral Rule.
    References:
  •  OMG - BMM - Directive
  •  OMG - SBVR - Element of Guidance
  •  OMG - UAF - Rule
  •  UCF Glossary - Directive
 		  Directive Building Block
  Information Object
An  Information Object  represents anything that can be communicated or memorized by an Agent in order to produce its Outcomes.
An  Information Object is either an Information Entity or a Information Property.
The difference lies in their relationship to change and to time. Information Entity(ies) can change over time and have a lifeycle while Information Propertys are immutable characteristics.
   Information Entity
  Policy
A  Policy is a Directive that is not directly enforceable whose purpose is to govern, guide or constrain the structure and behavior of Agents in the enterprise.
Policies provide the basis for rules and govern Behaviors carried out by Agents.
Example of  Policys are Regulation Articles, Business Policys or Architecture principles.
    References:
  •  OMG - BMM 1.3 - Business Policy
  •  OMG - UAF - Rule

It ranges from the different kidns of Directives (Business Policy, Architecture principle, Business Rule, etc.),  to classification of policies (Policy Category) up to an entire set of policies (Policy Dictionary). 		  Policy Block
  Policy Category  Policy Category is a classification of policies. In regulations, it represents sections of the law.

  Policy Dictionary A  Policy Dictionary is a set of Policy(ies), defined in laws published by governements or in policy frameworks defined by the enterprise.
Both Policy Frameworks and Regulatory Frameworks are  Policy Dictionary(ies).
