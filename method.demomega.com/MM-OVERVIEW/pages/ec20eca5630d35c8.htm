BUILDING BLOCK - Business Operating Context

DescriptionAbstract Concept
An  Business Operating Context is a perimeter of Architecture Block (Type) that forms the functional scope of an Enterprise. It can be a set of Business Operating Capability(ies) that define the purpose of Business Operating Agents or the Business Operating Ecosystem in which these Business Operating Agents deliver these Business Operating Capability(ies).
VersionUnder construction
Super Types  Operating Context  
Definition Domain  EA Pattern - Business Operating Pattern
The EA Pattern - Business Operating Pattern domain defines concepts and relationships used to model how a Business Operating Agent operates/functions in its Operating Eco-System to produce Business Outcomes.
