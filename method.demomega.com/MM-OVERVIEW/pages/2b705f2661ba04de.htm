BUILDING BLOCK - Business-Entity Ecosystem

DescriptionAbstract Concept
An  Business-Entity Ecosystem is a Resource Ecosystem used to describe the operating context in which a Business-Entity (Org-Unit) exists or lives for a specific purpose.
For instance, the operating context of a company includes its customers and suppliers.
VersionUnder construction
Super Types  Resource Ecosystem  
Definition Domain  Organization & Processes
The Organization & Processes domain is used to shape how business units operate to deliver the goods and services for which they are responsible.
