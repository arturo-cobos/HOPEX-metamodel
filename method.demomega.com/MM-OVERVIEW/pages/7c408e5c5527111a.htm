BUILDING BLOCK - Business Capability Map

Description
A  Business Capability Map is a top level assembly of Business Capability(ies) and their dependencies that, together, provide a Business Capability scope for an Enterprise Stage.
VersionAvailable since: HOPEX V1R2
Last Update: HOPEX V1R2
Super Types  Enterprise Capability Map  
Definition Domain  Enterprise Strategy & Roadmapping
The Enterprise Strategy & Roadmapping domain supports the process of analysing and optimising the delivery of Business Operating Capability(ies) in line with the Enterprise market analysis and strategic intent.
