BUILDING BLOCK - Business Environment

Description
A  Business Environment is an operating context which defines the interactions (Business Service Channel) of a Business-Entity (Org-Unit)  with its partners (Partner Org-Units).
VersionAvailable since: HOPEX V2
Last Update: HOPEX V2
