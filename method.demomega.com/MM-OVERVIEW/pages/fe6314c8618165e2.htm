BUILDING BLOCK - Business Object Assurance Case

Description
A  Business Object Assurance Case is a Data Assurance Case related to a critial Conceptual Object.
Its purpose is to defined the quality and integrity rules that apply to a Conceptual Object in each business contexts such as activity domains on the enterprise.
