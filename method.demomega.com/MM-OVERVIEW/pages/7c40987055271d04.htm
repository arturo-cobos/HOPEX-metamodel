BUILDING BLOCK - Conceptual Agent

DescriptionAbstract Concept
A  Conceptual Agent is a type of Business Operating Agent that depicts a functional division of labor within an enterprise, influencing the formation of its business operating model.
The concrete realizations of  Conceptual Agent come in the form of Activity Domain (a macro  Conceptual Agent) and Business Function (a mezzo  Conceptual Agent).
