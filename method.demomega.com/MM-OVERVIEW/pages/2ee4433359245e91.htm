DEFINITION - Enterprise Objective

Description
An Enterprise Objective is a quantifiable end that a company/organization wants to achieve for a given Enterprise Stage.
An Enterprise Objective may support an Enterprise Goal; it may be refined into sub-objectives.
An Enterprise Objective may concern a defined Exhibited Capability and be addressed by a defined Tactic.
