IDENTITY - Initiative Milestone

DescriptionAn  Initiative Milestone is a calendar change that marks the end of an Initiative and possibly the beginning of another.
Dictionary  Dictionary of HOPEX patterns
Super Types  Initiative Block  
Implementing MetaClass  Enterprise Event
Referencing Domain  Initiative and Transformation Pattern
The  Initiative and Transformation Pattern domain defines management concepts required for transformation initiatives.
It provides principles for the coordination of large scale initiatives evolving over time (Enduring Initiative and their Initiative Stages).
