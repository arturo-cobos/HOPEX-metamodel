Data Governance
The  Data Governance domain provides instruments that facilitate the management of data availability, usability, integrity, and security within an enterprise.
It covers three dimensions organized as sub-domains:
1) Privacy Assurance : mitigate risks on activities impacting privacy.
2) Data Provenance & Reliability Assurance : establish trust in the provenance of Information Objects.
3) Data Asset Assurance : establish trust in the quality of Information Objects.

  Business Process
An  Business Process is a set of Operations performed by Human Resources and/or by automated systems (Business Systems) to produce a Business Outcome. It is depicted as a sequence of Operations, controlled by events and conditions.
Operations are carried out by the involvment of Human Resources and system resources (Application) as participants in the process (Participant Resource Agent).
During its course of action, an  Business Process consumes or produces Data Objects.
1) It may read or write Data Objects in its Process Store.
2) It may receive Data Objects at its boundary:  Triggering Business Outcome.
3) It may produce Data Objects at its boundary:  Published Business Outcome.
The course of actions of an  Business Process is constrained by the application of rules (Business Rule Enforcement) that define what is allowed and not allowed to do.

  Business Partner

  Information Object
An  Information Object  represents anything that can be communicated or memorized by an Agent in order to produce its Outcomes.
An  Information Object is either an Information Entity or a Information Property.
The difference lies in their relationship to change and to time. Information Entity(ies) can change over time and have a lifeycle while Information Propertys are immutable characteristics.
   Information Entity
  Operational Risk Type
An  Operational Risk Type is a kind of Risk Type that refers to the potential for loss due to the enterprise's inadequate functioning.
An  Operational Risk Type is either Compliance Risk Type a or an Operating Risk Type.
