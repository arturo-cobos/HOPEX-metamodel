DEFINITION - Actor (UML)

DescriptionAn actor represents the role played by something or someone within the enterprise environment or the modeled system. It is related to the business activities of the enterprise, and interacts with the system in different use cases. It can be an element in the enterprise structure such as a division, a department, or a workstation.
