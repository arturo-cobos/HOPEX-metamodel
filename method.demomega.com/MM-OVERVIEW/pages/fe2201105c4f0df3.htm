BUILDING BLOCK - Business Directive

DescriptionAbstract Concept (Aspect)
A  Business Directive is Directive an intended to assert business structure or to control or influence the behavior of the enterprise.
 Business Directives are categorized as  Business Regulations and Conceptual Business Rules .
VersionAvailable since: MEGA 2009 SP5
Last Update: MEGA 2009 SP5
Super Types  Directive  
Definition Domain  EA Pattern - Business Operating Pattern
The EA Pattern - Business Operating Pattern domain defines concepts and relationships used to model how a Business Operating Agent operates/functions in its Operating Eco-System to produce Business Outcomes.
