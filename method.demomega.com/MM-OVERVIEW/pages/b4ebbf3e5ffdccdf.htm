BUILDING BLOCK - Resource Behavior

DescriptionAbstract Concept
A  Resource Behavior is a Resource Operating Asset that describes any action or reaction of a Resource Agent to external or internal circumstances. This includes Resource Processes (actions), Resource Interaction Scenarios (stories) or service interactions (Business Service Interface).
VersionUnder construction
Super Types  Business Behavior    Resource Operating Asset (Aspect)  
Definition Domain  EA Pattern - Business Resource Operating Pattern
The EA Pattern - Business Resource Operating Pattern domain defines concepts and relationships used to model how physical resources (Resource Operating Assets) operates/functions in Resource Ecosystems to deliver Business Outcomes.
