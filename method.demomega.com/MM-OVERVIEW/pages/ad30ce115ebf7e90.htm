Description

Classification of  concepts used to describe Enterprise Business Assets.
Business Assets are those that contributes to the delivery of Business Outcomes of the enterprise.
There are also classified by their level of granularity described in the Systemic Levels GRID.
The Operating Semantic describes the main relationship between these concepts starting from Capability offered by Agents that act and interact (Behavior) in their Operating Eco-Systems to produce Outcomes that benefit (value) to other Agents.
A complementary GRID (EA GRID - Technology Concepts) provides a classification of Technology Assets that supports Business Assets in their delivery of Business Outcomes.
