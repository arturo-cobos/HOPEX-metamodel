BUILDING BLOCK - Individual

DescriptionAbstract Concept
 Individuals are Entitys  which represent objects of the real world that exist over space and time. Examples are, the Oackland digital hospital in 2016, the registration process used at the Oackland digital hospital in 2021 or SAP S/4HANA deployed in Argentina since 2020.
 Individuals shall be made distinct from Class of Individual which they are instance of.
 Individuals are represented with light orange boxes (see the Meta-Model Legend ).
