BUILDING BLOCK - Resource Operating Asset

DescriptionAbstract Concept (Aspect)
 Resource Operating Assets comprise physical assets which contribute to the delivery of Business Outcomes of the enterprise. This includes Resource Agents, their behaviors (Resource Behavior:  Resource Process, Resource Interaction Scenario), and Natural Resources.
