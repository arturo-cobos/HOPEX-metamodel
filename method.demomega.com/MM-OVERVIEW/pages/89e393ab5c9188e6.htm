CONCEPT DOMAIN - DODAF2 - OV-2 - Operational Resource Flow Description

DescriptionThe OV-2 DoDAF-described Model applies the context of the operational capability to a community of anticipated users. The primary purpose of the OV-2 is to define capability requirements within an operational context. The OV-2 may also be used to express a capability boundary.

  Business Outcome
A  Business Outcome is a Business Event that indicates the availability of an expected change in the state of a Business Object, produced by a Business Operating Agent, for the benefits of an internal or external consumer (especially Customers).
Business Objects range from Information Objects to any organizational constructs (People & Accountability) or Resource Operating Assets offered, produced and delivered by an Enterprise.

  Business Scenario
A  Business Scenario is a story describing how Business-Entity (Org-Unit) interact wtiht its partners (Partner Org-Unit) to achieve Business Outcomes in a specific Eco-System (Business Environment).
This includes:
1) A course of events represented by  Business Object Flows depicting the story towards the delivery of expected Business Outcomes.
2) Interacting  Business Entities (Org-Units)  who participate to the story in the considered Eco-System.

An  Org-Unit is a type of administrative unit in government or business that is a template of Organization .
Example : Sales Department, Finance Department, Logistics Deparment.
