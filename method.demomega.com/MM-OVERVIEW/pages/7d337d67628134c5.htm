BUILDING BLOCK - Business Operating Scenario

DescriptionAbstract Concept
A  Business Operating Scenario is a story that describes how the components of an Business Operating Agent interacts to produce Business Outcomes.
This includes:
1) A course of events represented by  Business Object Flows depicting the steps towards the delivery of expected Business Outcomes.
2) Interacting Business Operating Agents who participate to the story.
VersionAvailable since: HOPEX V2
Last Update: HOPEX V2
Super Types  Business Behavior    Interaction Scenario  
Definition Domain  EA Pattern - Business Operating Pattern
The EA Pattern - Business Operating Pattern domain defines concepts and relationships used to model how a Business Operating Agent operates/functions in its Operating Eco-System to produce Business Outcomes.
