DEFINITION - Assessment Node

DescriptionObjects of this type are created either at assessment session deployment or at aggregation. Objects created at deployment are owned by the session and enable determination of the object that will be evaluated, by whom and where. It is also associated with values calculated from answers given in the assessor questionnaire for each of the assessed characteristics.
