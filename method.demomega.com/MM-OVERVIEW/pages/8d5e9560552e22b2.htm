BUILDING BLOCK - Policy Dictionary

DescriptionAbstract Concept
A  Policy Dictionary is a set of Policy(ies), defined in laws published by governements or in policy frameworks defined by the enterprise.
Both Policy Frameworks and Regulatory Frameworks are  Policy Dictionary(ies).
