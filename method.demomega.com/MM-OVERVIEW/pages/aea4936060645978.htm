BUILDING BLOCK - Business Operating Capability

SynonymEnterprise Capability
DescriptionAbstract Concept
A  Business Operating Capability is a Capability that benefits to Customers (internal or external) of the enterprise. It expresses an ability to deliver Business Outcomes.
This includes Business Capability, Functionality, Hardware Capability and Skill.
VersionAvailable since: HOPEX V2
Last Update: HOPEX V2
Super Types  Capability    Product Capability (Aspect)  
Definition Domain  EA Pattern - Business Operating Pattern
The EA Pattern - Business Operating Pattern domain defines concepts and relationships used to model how a Business Operating Agent operates/functions in its Operating Eco-System to produce Business Outcomes.
