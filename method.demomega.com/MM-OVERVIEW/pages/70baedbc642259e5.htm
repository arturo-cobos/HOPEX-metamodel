BUILDING BLOCK - Technology Object

DescriptionAbstract Concept (Aspect)
A  Technology Object represents any tangible or intanglible Technology System or state of such system, that can be the subject of a technology transaction.
As such, a  Technology Object can be improved or transformed in some way by a producer Technology System resulting in an increase in value (Technology Outcome) that benefits (utility) to a userTechnology System or Business System that use this Technology Object.
Examples:
- A new AI system that is the result of the development of a platform teamand sales activitiies of a software company (producer Agent) for the benefit of a Business System.
