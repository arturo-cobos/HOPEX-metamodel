BUILDING BLOCK - Concept

Description
A  Concept is the representation of any tangible or intanglible entity that is of interest to understand the enterprises, its data, resources and activities.
A  Concept is defined through its essential characteristics which can be:
1) A Concept Property that represents some an immutable factual characteristic such as "name", "amount".
2) A Concept Relationship that represents relationships to other  Concepts.
