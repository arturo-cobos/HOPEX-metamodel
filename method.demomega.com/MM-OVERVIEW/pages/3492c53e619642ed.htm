BUILDING BLOCK - Class of Individual

DescriptionAbstract Concept
A  Class of Individual is a Class whose members are instances of  Individual .
Example:
- Agent, Process, Capability
Counterexample:
- Project is not a  Class of Individual ibecause its instances have a spatio-temporal extent.
