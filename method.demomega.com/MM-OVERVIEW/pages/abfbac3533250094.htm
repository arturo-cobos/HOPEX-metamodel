DEFINITION - Business Process

Description
A business process represents a system that offers products or services to an internal or external client of the company or organization.
At higher levels, a business process represents a structure and a categorization of the business. It can be decomposed in other processes.
The link with organizational processes describes the actual implementation of the business process in the organization.
A business process can also be detailed by a functional view.
