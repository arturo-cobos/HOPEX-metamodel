BUILDING BLOCK - Appraisal

DescriptionAbstract Concept
An  Appraisal is a time bound statement expressed by stakeholders to qualify Architecture Blocks with respect to their worth.
Assessments and Decisions are two major types of  Appraisals provided by HOPEX.
VersionAvailable since: HOPEX V4.2
Last Update: HOPEX V4.2
Super Types  Governance Event  
Definition Domain  Appraisal Pattern
The Appraisal Pattern domain defines constructs used by stakeholders to asses and qualify Architecture Blocks.
