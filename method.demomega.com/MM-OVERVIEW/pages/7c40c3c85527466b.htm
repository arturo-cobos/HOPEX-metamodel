BUILDING BLOCK - Resource Process

DescriptionAbstract Concept
A  Resource Process is a Business Operating Process which is conducted by Resource Agents (physical resources such as Human Resource, System of Resources ,Business Systems) and which describes how these entities act to deliver Business Outcomes.
 Resource Processes include Business Processes and System Processes.
VersionAvailable since: MEGA 2009 SP3
Last Update: MEGA 2009 SP3
Super Types  Business Operating Process    Resource Behavior  
Definition Domain  EA Pattern - Business Resource Operating Pattern
The EA Pattern - Business Resource Operating Pattern domain defines concepts and relationships used to model how physical resources (Resource Operating Assets) operates/functions in Resource Ecosystems to deliver Business Outcomes.
