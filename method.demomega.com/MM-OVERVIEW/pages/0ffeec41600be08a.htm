BUILDING BLOCK - Initiative

DescriptionAbstract Concept
An  Initiative is any piece of work that is undertaken or attempted under the responsibility of a Steering Authority.
 Initiatives range from single project to long term initiatives (Enduring Initiative) such as Enterprises.
 Initiatives are sychronized over time using Initiative Milestones.

A plan recognized by an enterprise as being essential to achieving its goals - i.e. a strategic specification of what the enterprise does. In other words, a Course of Action channels efforts towards Desired Results.
Business Capabilities might be required by an Enterprise to conduct its Courses of Action.
