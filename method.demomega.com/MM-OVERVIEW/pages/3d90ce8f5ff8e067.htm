BUILDING BLOCK - Privacy Pre-Assessment

Description
This will help you determine your program or activity's potential privacy impacts and give you a sense of the risk level. The more privacy risk associated with your program or activity, the more you will need to analyze and mitigate the risk.
VersionAvailable since: HOPEX V2
Last Update: HOPEX V2
Super Types  Privacy Assessment  
Definition Domain  Privacy Assurance
Privacy assurance offers strategies and schemes adopted by an organization to prevent the flow of important information outside of the workplace. The approach includes investigation, remediation and reporting.
