BUILDING BLOCK - Technology System

DescriptionAbstract Concept
A  Technology System is an Agent which produces Technology Outcomes through Technology Interfaces.  Technology Systems are enabler of Business Systems. They do not directly deliver Business Outcomes to internal or external customers.
