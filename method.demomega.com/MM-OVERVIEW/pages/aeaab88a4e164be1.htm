DEFINITION - Strategy

DescriptionA Strategy is one component of the plan for the Mission. A Strategy represents the essential Course of Action to achieve Ends (Goals in particular). A Strategy usually channels efforts towards those Goals.
A  Strategy is accepted by the enterprise as the right approach to achieve its Goals, given the environmental constraints and risks.
