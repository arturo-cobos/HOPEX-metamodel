BUILDING BLOCK - Conceptual Interaction Scenario

Description
A  Conceptual Interaction Scenario is a story that describes how the components of an Enterprise Agent interacts to achieve Business Outcomes.
This includes:
1) A course of events represented by Business Object Flows depicting the steps towards the delivery of expected Business Outcomes.
2) Interacting Enterprise Agents who participate to the story.
