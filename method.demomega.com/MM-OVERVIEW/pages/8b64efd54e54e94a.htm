DEFINITION - Assessed Characteristics

DescriptionThe assessed characteristic defines what the assessment seeks to assess. It can be associated with a MetaClass, and more specifically with one of its MetaAttributes. For example, MetaClass Risk, MetaAttribute: LikeLihood. An assessment session can handle several assessed characteristics.
